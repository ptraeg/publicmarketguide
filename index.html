    Public Market Guide

    This application provides information about markets, vendors, events, and eateries for farmer's markets in the Monroe County, NY area. The application is supported on both the iOS and Android platforms. Native device features such as maps, email, and the phone dialer are easily accessed through entries for vendors, markets, etc.

    About The Application

    This application was developed by Nick Evevsky and Peter Traeg (@ptraeg) for the AT&T Rochester Civic App Challenge.
